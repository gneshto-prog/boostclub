Ambassador Programme

We're not looking for salespeople.
We're looking for people who invest in themselves.

The Boost Club Ambassador Programme is for people who first want to change themselves — and only then help others do the same. It's selective, structured and based on real results, not promises.

Who it's for

The programme is for you if…

✓ Right for you if

  • ✓ You're already a Boost Club client with proven personal results
  • ✓ You want to learn a craft — wellness, nutrition, body composition assessment
  • ✓ You believe in what you've experienced and want to share it authentically
  • ✓ You're willing to invest time in your own learning before helping others

✗ Not right if

  • ✗ You're looking for quick income or a guaranteed second salary
  • ✗ You haven't personally gone through a process of change
  • ✗ You expect a script and a commission structure
  • ✗ You don't believe in the products or the process from personal experience
The process

What the journey looks like

There are no shortcuts. There is a process.

1

Free consultation — you as a client

Everything starts with your own body assessment. You come as a regular client. You measure, you understand, you begin. You can't guide someone else if you haven't been through the process yourself.

2

Observation period (4–8 weeks)

You're present at consultations, you listen, you ask questions. You build your own results. Gabriel assesses whether you have the right attitude and mindset — not sales skills.

3

Practical training

You learn how bioimpedance assessment works, how to read body composition data, how to build a personalised plan. The training is hands-on, not from a manual.

4

First assisted consultations

You lead your first consultations with Gabriel present. You receive direct, unvarnished feedback. If it's not right, you redo it. Standards don't drop.

5

Gradual autonomy

When results and attitude confirm you're ready, you receive more independence. The pace is yours — there's no pressure to advance faster than you're comfortable with.

What you build

What you build in this programme

Real knowledge

You understand the human body, body composition, nutrition and behaviour — not just what to sell.

Credibility

You have personal results, real training, and the trust of clients who see you as a partner, not a salesperson.

A business of your own

The Herbalife Independent Distributor model allows you to build a sustainable income based on your own clients and network — on your own terms.
