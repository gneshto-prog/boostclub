How you learn

The "do to learn" philosophy

No endless theory and no rigid timelines. You do from the start — and you learn exactly what you do.

1

Send your request

Choose how you want to start — part-time or full-time. Gabriel receives your request and contacts you for a first conversation.

2

Observation through practice

From day one you do, you don't just watch: you take part in consultations, work with the community, and learn how the club runs. Gabriel is beside you to help you learn and do everything — at your own pace, with no fixed deadlines.

3

Build

With the support of a global community of partners who teach, collaborate and build together — from your first client to your own club.
