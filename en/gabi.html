The story, in short

I built my own body first. Then a club.

I started out underweight, without structure and with confidence near zero. Proper nutrition and consistent training added over 20 kg of muscle — and showed me that results don't come from information, but from a system, simplicity and people beside you.

Today I compete as a semi-pro MMA fighter and track athlete, and the discipline from the ring is the same one I bring to the club: measurable steps, executed consistently, no shortcuts.

I'm the third generation of a family working in wellness since 1989 — thousands of people guided, across dozens of countries. Boost Club, at Sevastopol 24, is where all that experience comes home.

"Information alone isn't enough. You need a system, simplicity and people beside you. That changed everything for me — and it's exactly what I'm building for my clients."
