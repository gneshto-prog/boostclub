The story

How Gabriel got here

I grew up in a family where wellness wasn't a trend — it was a way of life. My grandparents and parents have worked in this field for 36 years. From a young age I saw people who came to us without direction and left with clarity. I saw real transformations: people who lost weight healthily, people who gained energy, people who finally understood what they were eating and why.

But I also experienced firsthand how hard it can be. At one point, despite having grown up surrounded by this knowledge, I reached a point where I didn't feel good in my own body. I put on weight, I lacked energy and I didn't know where to start — even me, with all that heritage behind me.

"I understood then that information alone isn't enough. You need someone to translate it into concrete steps for your real life. That's where everything started."

— Gabriel Neshto

I decided to work seriously: proper nutrition, consistent training, real habits — not extreme diets. I lost 20 kg and, more importantly, I truly understood the process from the inside. Not as theory, but as lived experience.

I followed the ISSA certification because I wanted to combine family heritage with rigorous, up-to-date training. And I opened Boost Club because I wanted to do for the people of Bucharest what my family has done for 36 years: help them start with the right data and a realistic plan.

Family heritage

36 years of wellness — a tradition that continues

The Neshto family has worked in nutrition and wellness since 1989. Over these decades, they have guided thousands of people — of all ages, with all types of goals. Gabriel is the natural continuation of this tradition, with a younger, more modern perspective closer to the pace of life today.

This means that behind Boost Club stands not an entrepreneur who read a few books — but a family that has seen what works on thousands of real people, in real time, for nearly four decades.

Philosophy

How Gabriel works

There are no templates. There are people — each with their own rhythm, goals and life.

Realistic, not idealist

Perfect plans that can't be kept help no one. Gabriel builds recommendations you can follow in your real life — not the Instagram version of your life.

Supported, not judged

It doesn't matter where you start. There's no minimum fitness level or "right" body to come to a consultation. Every person deserves clarity and support, regardless of starting point.

Consistent, not dramatic

Lasting transformations are built through small habits repeated consistently — not through radical changes impossible to maintain. This is the central philosophy of Boost Club.
