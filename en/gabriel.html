Philosophy

The 3 S philosophy: Systems, Simplicity, Support

Health doesn't run on willpower. It runs on three things built correctly.

Systems

A good system removes the need to make decisions. If after a long day at work you have to choose between healthy food and junk food, you'll choose wrong. When the system chooses for you, the moment of weakness never comes.

Simplicity

If your "healthy" breakfast takes an hour in the morning, half-asleep, plus a mountain of dishes — you'll end up doing neither. What's simple gets done. What's complicated gets postponed.

Support

The best students in the world have teachers. The best athletes have coaches. The best doctors have mentors. Yet most people think health is something to figure out alone. News flash: you don't have to.
