What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember certain preferences or to function correctly.

Cookies we use

TypePurposeRequires consent?
Strictly necessaryOperation of the booking form, browsing session, basic securityNo — required for operation
PreferencesRemembering navigation preferences (e.g. menu open/closed)No — functional, no tracking
Analytics (Google Analytics)Anonymised visitor statistics — how many people visit the site, which pages are popularYes — activated only with your consent
Marketing / advertisingTracking for personalised adsWe do not use such cookies

Google Analytics

If you consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how the site is used. Data is anonymised (IP masked) and does not identify individuals. You can opt out at any time via your browser cookie settings or the official Google Analytics Opt-out add-on.

How to control cookies

You can manage or delete cookies from your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect the operation of the booking form.
