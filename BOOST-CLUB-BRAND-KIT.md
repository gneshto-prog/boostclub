# Boost Club — Brand Kit (for Canva)

Pulled directly from the live site's `css/style.css`. Enter in Canva: **Brand Hub → Brand Kit → Colors / Fonts / Logos**.
(First, delete the stray `#a7d28a` swatch already in the palette.)

## Colors
In Canva: Colors → "Add new" → type the hex (without the #) → Enter. Repeat for each.

**Primary**
- `0D5645` — Brand Green (main / backgrounds, headings)
- `C9A24B` — Gold (accent, buttons, highlights)

**Supporting greens**
- `11705A` — Green Mid
- `0A4537` — Green Dark
- `08382D` — Green Deep (deepest)

**Soft accent**
- `E0BE6E` — Gold Soft (hover / lighter gold)

**Neutrals**
- `E6E9DE` — Sage (soft panels)
- `F5F3EE` — Cream (page background)
- `FFFFFF` — White (cards)
- `1A1F1C` — Ink (body text, near-black)
- `5C6660` — Slate (muted/secondary text)

**Utility**
- `25D366` — WhatsApp Green (use only for WhatsApp buttons)

## Fonts
The whole site uses **Poppins** (it's built into Canva — search "Poppins").
- **Headings:** Poppins — ExtraBold (800) or Bold (700)
- **Body:** Poppins — Regular (400) or Medium (500)

## Logo
- Wordmark: **"Boost Club."** — the two "o"s are gold leaf shapes, and the final period is **gold** (`C9A24B`).
- Leaf: gold body with a green-deep (`08382D`) diagonal stroke.
- **Tagline:** "Be your best"
- On dark green backgrounds: white/cream text with gold leaves. On light: dark text with green leaves.
